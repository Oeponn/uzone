Favorite Clothing Brands


  • "Cav Empt" by Toby Feltwell & Sk8thing

    A Japanese brand whose name is short for the Latin phrase 'Caveat Emptor' which means buyer beware. Would have come up in economics class. Good quality, boxy cuts, retro graphics. A lot of it is really ugly lol.

  • "Julius_7" by Tatsuro Horikawa

    Very dark goth brand. I mostly like their pants with a lot of cargo pockets. Nicknamed 'diaper pants' because they tend to be very dropcrotched.

  • "Undercover" by Jun Takahashi

    Another Japanese designer. Early on had a lot of punk references. Scab 03SS season in particular had a lot of focus on the crust trend. Has done a lot of collabs with franchises like Evangelion or Stanley Kubrick. Also very hit or miss with some absurdly high pricepoints.
